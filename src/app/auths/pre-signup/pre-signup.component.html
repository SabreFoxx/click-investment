Open an account

Things you'll need

  • Your email account and phone number

  • Your crypto wallet address (to fund your account)

New account process

  1. Fill in the registration form

    Enter your details in the registration form. Afterwhich, click submit to proceed.

  2. Select a payment method and plan

    Select the method by which you'll fund your account, and also a plan which you'll fund into.

  3. Start investing

    Once your account is funded, you can monitor your daily investment balance and earn.
